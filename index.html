Washington Wizards

The Washington Wizards are an American professional basketball team based in Washington, D.C.
The Wizards compete in the National Basketball Association (NBA) as a member of the Southeast Division of the Eastern Conference.
The team plays its home games at Capital One Arena, in the Chinatown
neighborhood of Washington, D.C. an arena they share with the Washington Capitals of the National Hockey League (NHL) and the Georgetown University men's basketball team. The team is owned by Ted Leonsis through Monumental Sports & Entertainment.
